## 🔑 重点句子解析

### 1. [00:30] Becca

**原文**: Well, I'd rather be too hot and cool down than too cold and try to warm up.

**解析**: 使用"would rather...than..."结构，表示宁愿...也不愿...。表达了对寒冷的不适。

**关键词**: would rather A than B, cool down, warm up

### 2. [01:15] Phil

**原文**: Our experience of cold depends on many things, including our genes, culture and place of birth.

**解析**: 引出主题：寒冷感受受多种因素影响。

**关键词**: depend on, including, genes, place of birth

### 3. [02:20] Gunhild

**原文**: First and foremost, I would say that gender differs. Males often are more tolerant, perhaps, than females.

**解析**: 引出第一个因素：性别差异。展示如何用英语表达观点。

**关键词**: first and foremost, tolerant of, gender

### 4. [03:45] Dr Cara

**原文**: Yes and no. The more data we collect, the more confusing the picture gets.

**解析**: 展示如何用"yes and no"表达模棱两可的答案。

**关键词**: yes and no, the more...the more..., confusing

### 5. [04:30] Phil

**原文**: Subjectively, the herders manage better. They do not feel so cold.

**解析**: 对比客观事实和主观感受。

**关键词**: subjectively, manage, 客观 vs 主观
